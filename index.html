Who Owns Denver?

What is this?

This is a map of every property that is owned by a landlord. I have defined a landlord as someone who owns more than 1 property or has LLC or Trust in the name of the owner. This map should help illustrate how much property in Denver is owned as "an investment".

How to use

Click on a marker to view the address, owner name, and the other properties that person owns.

How can I verify this information?

All of this information is publicly available and verifiable at https://www.denvergov.org/property/realproperty/

Things of Note

  • Since this list is compiled by owner, there are owners that some people may not typically consider landlords. Owners like the "CITY & COUNTY OF DENVER" or "SCHOOL DISTRICT NO 1". I left those in the data set because I didn't want to accidentally remove one that sounds like it is a government entity when really it is a private entity.
  • Government entities are marked using a red circle. Currently that only includes the "CITY & COUNTY OF DENVER" and "UNITED STATES OF AMERICA" but more can be added to that list if necessary

Mile High View

02/18/2021: After a first pass it appears that between 25% - 33% of home on any given block in Denver are rentals. Which would mean that 1 out of every 3 or 4 Denverites are renters

Why did you make this?

While living and working in Denver my partner and I have finally decided we would like to buy a house. We have been renting until we had enough money saved up. My partner had heard that we would be competing against investors and landlords. I was interested in learning how many properties in Denver were in fact owned by investors and landlords so I figured this would be a neat project. Little did I know how pervasive investor and landlord owned properties were.
